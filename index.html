How It Works

Aery enables riders and drivers to find each other to split transportation costs

Easily search for a ride by clicking “Search Rides” and filter to find a ride that works for you. Drivers can fill out the “Driver Form” and input info like route, time, and $ per passenger. What makes Aery unique is that we can automatically pair you. Once a rider submits a “Rider Form” an algorithm will search all viable drivers and routes to find one that works. Every new form submission will be searched to make sure drivers have a full car, and riders have a way to get home. All data is secure and anonymous so only the approved driver will have access to your trip. Let us do the work for you, Ride Aery.
